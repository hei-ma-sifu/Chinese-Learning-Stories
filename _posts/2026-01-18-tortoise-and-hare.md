# 龟兔赛跑 
## The Tortoise and the Hare

---

### 故事开始 (The Story Begins)

**从前，有一只兔子和一只乌龟。**  
*Cóngqián, yǒu yì zhī tùzi hé yì zhī wūguī.*  
Once upon a time, there was a rabbit and a tortoise.

**兔子跑得很快，乌龟爬得很慢。**  
*Tùzi pǎo de hěn kuài, wūguī pá de hěn màn.*  
The rabbit ran very fast, the tortoise crawled very slowly.

**兔子总是笑话乌龟："你太慢了！哈哈哈！"**  
*Tùzi zǒngshì xiàohua wūguī: "Nǐ tài màn le! Hāhāhā!"*  
The rabbit always made fun of the tortoise: "You're too slow! Hahaha!"

---

### 比赛开始 (The Race Begins)

**乌龟说："我们来比赛吧！"**  
*Wūguī shuō: "Wǒmen lái bǐsài ba!"*  
The tortoise said: "Let's have a race!"

**"好啊！"兔子说，"我一定赢！"**  
*"Hǎo a!" Tùzi shuō, "Wǒ yídìng yíng!"*  
"Sure!" said the rabbit, "I'll definitely win!"

**比赛开始了！**  
*Bǐsài kāishǐ le!*  
The race started!

**兔子跑得像风一样快。**  
*Tùzi pǎo de xiàng fēng yíyàng kuài.*  
The rabbit ran as fast as the wind.

**乌龟爬得一步一步，慢慢地向前走。**  
*Wūguī pá de yí bù yí bù, mànmàn de xiàng qián zǒu.*  
The tortoise crawled step by step, slowly moving forward.

---

### 兔子睡觉了 (The Rabbit Falls Asleep)

**兔子跑到一半，回头一看。**  
*Tùzi pǎo dào yíbàn, huítóu yí kàn.*  
The rabbit ran halfway, then looked back.

**"乌龟还在后面呢！我先睡一觉吧。"**  
*"Wūguī hái zài hòumiàn ne! Wǒ xiān shuì yí jiào ba."*  
"The tortoise is still way behind! I'll take a nap first."

**兔子在树下睡着了。**  
*Tùzi zài shù xià shuìzháo le.*  
The rabbit fell asleep under a tree.

**乌龟继续爬，一步一步，不停地爬。**  
*Wūguī jìxù pá, yí bù yí bù, bù tíng de pá.*  
The tortoise kept crawling, step by step, crawling without stopping.

---

### 谁赢了？(Who Won?)

**乌龟爬过了兔子，到了终点！**  
*Wūguī pá guò le tùzi, dào le zhōngdiǎn!*  
The tortoise crawled past the rabbit and reached the finish line!

**兔子醒了，快快跑到终点。**  
*Tùzi xǐng le, kuàikuài pǎo dào zhōngdiǎn.*  
The rabbit woke up and quickly ran to the finish line.

**可是，太晚了！**  
*Kěshì, tài wǎn le!*  
But it was too late!

**"我赢了！"乌龟高兴地说。**  
*"Wǒ yíng le!" Wūguī gāoxìng de shuō.*  
"I won!" said the tortoise happily.

**兔子很不好意思："对不起，我太骄傲了。"**  
*Tùzi hěn bù hǎoyìsi: "Duìbuqǐ, wǒ tài jiāo'ào le."*  
The rabbit felt embarrassed: "I'm sorry, I was too proud."

---

### 故事的道理 (The Moral of the Story)

**慢慢来，不要放弃，就能成功！**  
*Màn man lái, búyào fàngqì, jiù néng chénggōng!*  
Go slowly, don't give up, and you can succeed!

**骄傲使人失败，努力让人成功！**  
*Jiāo'ào shǐ rén shībài, nǔlì ràng rén chénggōng!*  
Pride leads to failure, effort leads to success!
