# 皇帝的新衣
## The Emperor's New Clothes

---

### 爱穿新衣的皇帝 (The Emperor Who Loved New Clothes)

**很久很久以前，有一个国家。**  
*Hěn jiǔ hěn jiǔ yǐqián, yǒu yí gè guójiā.*  
A long, long time ago, there was a country.

**这个国家的皇帝非常爱穿漂亮的衣服。**  
*Zhège guójiā de huángdì fēicháng ài chuān piàoliang de yīfu.*  
The emperor of this country loved to wear beautiful clothes.

**他每天都要换好几次衣服。**  
*Tā měitiān dōu yào huàn hǎo jǐ cì yīfu.*  
He changed clothes several times every day.

**他的衣柜里有上千件华丽的衣服！**  
*Tā de yīguì lǐ yǒu shàng qiān jiàn huálì de yīfu!*  
His wardrobe had over a thousand magnificent outfits!

**皇帝把所有的钱都花在买衣服上。**  
*Huángdì bǎ suǒyǒu de qián dōu huā zài mǎi yīfu shàng.*  
The emperor spent all his money on buying clothes.

**他对国家大事一点儿也不关心。**  
*Tā duì guójiā dàshì yìdiǎnr yě bù guānxīn.*  
He didn't care about important matters of state at all.

---

### 两个骗子来了 (Two Swindlers Arrived)

**有一天，两个骗子来到了这个国家。**  
*Yǒu yì tiān, liǎng gè piànzi lái dào le zhège guójiā.*  
One day, two swindlers came to this country.

**他们听说皇帝爱穿新衣服。**  
*Tāmen tīngshuō huángdì ài chuān xīn yīfu.*  
They heard that the emperor loved to wear new clothes.

**"我们有办法赚很多钱！"他们互相说。**  
*"Wǒmen yǒu bànfǎ zhuàn hěn duō qián!" Tāmen hùxiāng shuō.*  
"We have a way to make a lot of money!" they said to each other.

**骗子们去见皇帝。**  
*Piànzi men qù jiàn huángdì.*  
The swindlers went to see the emperor.

**"陛下，我们是世界上最好的裁缝！"**  
*"Bìxià, wǒmen shì shìjiè shàng zuì hǎo de cáiféng!"*  
"Your Majesty, we are the best tailors in the world!"

**"我们能织出最神奇的布料！"**  
*"Wǒmen néng zhī chū zuì shénqí de bùliào!"*  
"We can weave the most magical fabric!"

**"这种布料非常特别。"**  
*"Zhè zhǒng bùliào fēicháng tèbié."*  
"This fabric is very special."

**"聪明的人能看见它，愚蠢的人看不见！"**  
*"Cōngming de rén néng kàn jiàn tā, yúchǔn de rén kàn bú jiàn!"*  
"Smart people can see it, but foolish people cannot!"

---

### 皇帝很高兴 (The Emperor Was Delighted)

**皇帝听了非常高兴。**  
*Huángdì tīng le fēicháng gāoxìng.*  
The emperor was very delighted to hear this.

**"太好了！如果我穿上这种衣服，"**  
*"Tài hǎo le! Rúguǒ wǒ chuān shàng zhè zhǒng yīfu,"*  
"Wonderful! If I wear this kind of clothes,"

**"我就能知道谁聪明，谁愚蠢！"**  
*"Wǒ jiù néng zhīdào shéi cōngming, shéi yúchǔn!"*  
"I'll be able to know who's smart and who's foolish!"

**皇帝给了骗子们很多金子和丝绸。**  
*Huángdì gěi le piànzi men hěn duō jīnzi hé sīchóu.*  
The emperor gave the swindlers lots of gold and silk.

**"快开始工作吧！"**  
*"Kuài kāishǐ gōngzuò ba!"*  
"Start working quickly!"

**骗子们装模作样地开始"织布"。**  
*Piànzi men zhuāng mú zuò yàng de kāishǐ "zhī bù".*  
The swindlers pretended to start "weaving."

**其实，织布机上什么都没有！**  
*Qíshí, zhī bù jī shàng shénme dōu méiyǒu!*  
In fact, there was nothing on the loom!

**他们把金子和丝绸都偷偷藏进了自己的口袋。**  
*Tāmen bǎ jīnzi hé sīchóu dōu tōutōu cáng jìn le zìjǐ de kǒudài.*  
They secretly hid all the gold and silk in their own pockets.

---

### 大臣去检查 (The Minister Went to Inspect)

**过了几天，皇帝派老大臣去看看。**  
*Guò le jǐ tiān, huángdì pài lǎo dàchén qù kànkan.*  
After a few days, the emperor sent an old minister to take a look.

**大臣来到织布房。**  
*Dàchén lái dào zhī bù fáng.*  
The minister came to the weaving room.

**他睁大眼睛仔细看。**  
*Tā zhēng dà yǎnjīng zǐxì kàn.*  
He opened his eyes wide and looked carefully.

**"天哪！我什么都看不见！"他心里想。**  
*"Tiān nǎ! Wǒ shénme dōu kàn bú jiàn!" Tā xīnli xiǎng.*  
"Oh my! I can't see anything!" he thought to himself.

**"难道我是愚蠢的人吗？"**  
*"Nándào wǒ shì yúchǔn de rén ma?"*  
"Could it be that I'm a foolish person?"

**但是他害怕别人说他愚蠢。**  
*Dànshì tā hàipà biérén shuō tā yúchǔn.*  
But he was afraid others would say he was foolish.

**"陛下，布料美极了！"大臣撒谎说。**  
*"Bìxià, bùliào měi jí le!" Dàchén sāhuǎng shuō.*  
"Your Majesty, the fabric is extremely beautiful!" the minister lied.

**"颜色鲜艳，图案精致！"**  
*"Yánsè xiānyàn, tú'àn jīngzhì!"*  
"The colors are bright, the patterns are exquisite!"

---

### 皇帝试穿新衣 (The Emperor Tries On the New Clothes)

**终于，"新衣"做好了。**  
*Zhōngyú, "xīn yī" zuò hǎo le.*  
Finally, the "new clothes" were finished.

**骗子们请皇帝来试穿。**  
*Piànzi men qǐng huángdì lái shì chuān.*  
The swindlers invited the emperor to try them on.

**"陛下，请脱下旧衣服吧。"**  
*"Bìxià, qǐng tuō xià jiù yīfu ba."*  
"Your Majesty, please take off your old clothes."

**皇帝脱下了所有的衣服。**  
*Huángdì tuō xià le suǒyǒu de yīfu.*  
The emperor took off all his clothes.

**骗子们假装给他穿上"新衣"。**  
*Piànzi men jiǎzhuāng gěi tā chuān shàng "xīn yī".*  
The swindlers pretended to dress him in the "new clothes."

**"陛下，您看！多么合身啊！"**  
*"Bìxià, nín kàn! Duōme héshēn a!"*  
"Your Majesty, look! How well it fits!"

**皇帝看着镜子。**  
*Huángdì kàn zhe jìngzi.*  
The emperor looked in the mirror.

**他什么也看不见！**  
*Tā shénme yě kàn bú jiàn!*  
He couldn't see anything at all!

**"我难道是愚蠢的人吗？"皇帝心里害怕极了。**  
*"Wǒ nándào shì yúchǔn de rén ma?" Huángdì xīnli hàipà jí le.*  
"Could it be that I'm a foolish person?" The emperor was extremely frightened inside.

**"不！我不能让大家知道！"**  
*"Bù! Wǒ bù néng ràng dàjiā zhīdào!"*  
"No! I cannot let everyone know!"

**"太美了！这是我见过最漂亮的衣服！"皇帝大声说。**  
*"Tài měi le! Zhè shì wǒ jiàn guò zuì piàoliang de yīfu!" Huángdì dàshēng shuō.*  
"So beautiful! This is the most beautiful outfit I've ever seen!" the emperor said loudly.

---

### 游行开始 (The Parade Begins)

**皇帝决定穿着"新衣"在大街上游行。**  
*Huángdì juédìng chuān zhe "xīn yī" zài dàjiē shàng yóuxíng.*  
The emperor decided to parade through the streets wearing his "new clothes."

**所有的百姓都站在街道两旁。**  
*Suǒyǒu de bǎixìng dōu zhàn zài jiēdào liǎng páng.*  
All the common people stood on both sides of the street.

**大家都想看看神奇的新衣。**  
*Dàjiā dōu xiǎng kànkan shénqí de xīn yī.*  
Everyone wanted to see the magical new clothes.

**皇帝走了出来。**  
*Huángdì zǒu le chū lái.*  
The emperor walked out.

**其实，他什么都没穿！**  
*Qíshí, tā shénme dōu méi chuān!*  
In fact, he wasn't wearing anything at all!

**但是没有人敢说出来。**  
*Dànshì méiyǒu rén gǎn shuō chū lái.*  
But no one dared to speak out.

**"天哪！皇帝的新衣真漂亮！"有人喊。**  
*"Tiān nǎ! Huángdì de xīn yī zhēn piàoliang!" Yǒurén hǎn.*  
"Oh my! The emperor's new clothes are so beautiful!" someone shouted.

**"是啊！太华丽了！"另一个人附和道。**  
*"Shì a! Tài huálì le!" Lìng yí gè rén fùhè dào.*  
"Yes! So magnificent!" another person chimed in.

**大家都假装能看见新衣。**  
*Dàjiā dōu jiǎzhuāng néng kàn jiàn xīn yī.*  
Everyone pretended they could see the new clothes.

**因为没有人想被认为是愚蠢的。**  
*Yīnwèi méiyǒu rén xiǎng bèi rènwéi shì yúchǔn de.*  
Because no one wanted to be considered foolish.

---

### 小孩说出了真相 (A Child Told the Truth)

**这时，一个小孩子的声音响起来了。**  
*Zhè shí, yí gè xiǎo háizi de shēngyīn xiǎng qǐlái le.*  
At this moment, a child's voice rang out.

**"可是皇帝什么都没穿啊！"**  
*"Kěshì huángdì shénme dōu méi chuān a!"*  
"But the emperor isn't wearing anything at all!"

**小孩子天真地说。**  
*Xiǎo háizi tiānzhēn de shuō.*  
The child said innocently.

**"嘘！不要乱说！"孩子的父亲紧张地说。**  
*"Xū! Búyào luàn shuō!" Háizi de fùqin jǐnzhāng de shuō.*  
"Shh! Don't speak nonsense!" the child's father said nervously.

**但是其他人都听见了。**  
*Dànshì qítā rén dōu tīng jiàn le.*  
But the others all heard.

**"对啊！皇帝什么都没穿！"**  
*"Duì a! Huángdì shénme dōu méi chuān!"*  
"That's right! The emperor isn't wearing anything!"

**"小孩子说的是真话！"**  
*"Xiǎo háizi shuō de shì zhēnhuà!"*  
"The child is telling the truth!"

**消息很快传开了。**  
*Xiāoxi hěn kuài chuán kāi le.*  
The news spread quickly.

---

### 故事的道理 (The Moral of the Story)

**不要因为害怕别人的看法而不敢说真话。**  
*Búyào yīnwèi hàipà biérén de kànfǎ ér bù gǎn shuō zhēnhuà.*  
Don't be afraid to tell the truth because you fear others' opinions.

**诚实和勇气比虚荣更重要。**  
*Chéngshí hé yǒngqì bǐ xūróng gèng zhòngyào.*  
Honesty and courage are more important than vanity.

**有时候，孩子的天真能看穿大人的谎言。**  
*Yǒushíhou, háizi de tiānzhēn néng kàn chuān dàrén de huǎngyán.*  
Sometimes, a child's innocence can see through adults' lies.

**要相信自己的眼睛，勇敢说出真相！**  
*Yào xiāngxìn zìjǐ de yǎnjīng, yǒnggǎn shuō chū zhēnxiàng!*  
Believe your own eyes and bravely speak the truth!
