# 丑小鸭
## The Ugly Duckling

---

### 春天来了 (Spring Arrived)

**春天到了，天气很暖和。**  
*Chūntiān dào le, tiānqì hěn nuǎnhuo.*  
Spring arrived, and the weather was very warm.

**在农场里，一只鸭妈妈正在孵蛋。**  
*Zài nóngchǎng lǐ, yì zhī yā māma zhèngzài fū dàn.*  
On the farm, a mother duck was hatching eggs.

**"咔嚓！咔嚓！"蛋壳裂开了。**  
*"Kā chā! Kā chā!" Dànké liè kāi le.*  
"Crack! Crack!" The eggshells broke open.

**一只、两只、三只小鸭子出来了！**  
*Yì zhī, liǎng zhī, sān zhī xiǎo yāzi chū lái le!*  
One, two, three little ducklings came out!

**它们又黄又可爱。**  
*Tāmen yòu huáng yòu kě'ài.*  
They were yellow and adorable.

**可是，还有一个很大的蛋。**  
*Kěshì, hái yǒu yí gè hěn dà de dàn.*  
But there was still one very big egg.

---

### 不一样的小鸭 (The Different Duckling)

**"咔嚓！"最后一个蛋也裂开了。**  
*"Kā chā!" Zuìhòu yí gè dàn yě liè kāi le.*  
"Crack!" The last egg also broke open.

**出来了一只小鸭子。**  
*Chū lái le yì zhī xiǎo yāzi.*  
A duckling came out.

**可是，这只小鸭子长得很不一样！**  
*Kěshì, zhè zhī xiǎo yāzi zhǎng de hěn bù yíyàng!*  
But this duckling looked very different!

**它又大又灰，一点儿也不可爱。**  
*Tā yòu dà yòu huī, yìdiǎnr yě bù kě'ài.*  
It was big and gray, not cute at all.

**"这是我的孩子吗？"鸭妈妈想，"它真丑啊！"**  
*"Zhè shì wǒ de háizi ma?" Yā māma xiǎng, "Tā zhēn chǒu a!"*  
"Is this my child?" Mother duck thought, "It's so ugly!"

---

### 大家都不喜欢它 (Nobody Liked It)

**其他小鸭子都不跟它玩。**  
*Qítā xiǎo yāzi dōu bù gēn tā wán.*  
The other ducklings didn't play with it.

**"你太丑了！走开！"**  
*"Nǐ tài chǒu le! Zǒu kāi!"*  
"You're too ugly! Go away!"

**农场里的鸡、鹅也欺负它。**  
*Nóngchǎng lǐ de jī, é yě qīfu tā.*  
The chickens and geese on the farm also bullied it.

**"丑小鸭！丑小鸭！"它们叫着。**  
*"Chǒu xiǎo yā! Chǒu xiǎo yā!" Tāmen jiào zhe.*  
"Ugly duckling! Ugly duckling!" they called out.

**小鸭子很伤心，也很孤单。**  
*Xiǎo yāzi hěn shāngxīn, yě hěn gūdān.*  
The duckling was very sad and lonely.

**"没有人喜欢我。我要离开这里。"**  
*"Méiyǒu rén xǐhuan wǒ. Wǒ yào líkāi zhèlǐ."*  
"Nobody likes me. I'm going to leave here."

---

### 孤单的冬天 (A Lonely Winter)

**小鸭子离开了农场，一个人生活。**  
*Xiǎo yāzi líkāi le nóngchǎng, yí gè rén shēnghuó.*  
The duckling left the farm and lived alone.

**夏天过去了，秋天来了。**  
*Xiàtiān guòqù le, qiūtiān lái le.*  
Summer passed, and autumn came.

**秋天过去了，冬天来了。**  
*Qiūtiān guòqù le, dōngtiān lái le.*  
Autumn passed, and winter came.

**冬天很冷很冷。**  
*Dōngtiān hěn lěng hěn lěng.*  
Winter was very, very cold.

**小鸭子又冷又饿，非常可怜。**  
*Xiǎo yāzi yòu lěng yòu è, fēicháng kělián.*  
The duckling was cold and hungry, very pitiful.

**"我真想有一个家。"它想。**  
*"Wǒ zhēn xiǎng yǒu yí gè jiā." tā xiǎng.*  
"I really wish I had a home," it thought.

---

### 美丽的天鹅 (Beautiful Swans)

**一天，小鸭子看见一群白色的大鸟。**  
*Yì tiān, xiǎo yāzi kàn jiàn yì qún báisè de dà niǎo.*  
One day, the duckling saw a flock of big white birds.

**它们在天上飞，又白又美丽。**  
*Tāmen zài tiān shàng fēi, yòu bái yòu měilì.*  
They were flying in the sky, white and beautiful.

**"它们是天鹅！多美啊！"**  
*"Tāmen shì tiān'é! Duō měi a!"*  
"They're swans! How beautiful!"

**"我真希望我也能那么美丽。"**  
*"Wǒ zhēn xīwàng wǒ yě néng nàme měilì."*  
"I really wish I could be that beautiful too."

---

### 春天又来了 (Spring Came Again)

**漫长的冬天终于过去了。**  
*Màncháng de dōngtiān zhōngyú guòqù le.*  
The long winter finally passed.

**春天又来了！**  
*Chūntiān yòu lái le!*  
Spring came again!

**一天，它来到湖边。**  
*Yì tiān, tā lái dào hú biān.*  
One day, it came to the lakeside.

**它看见水里有一只大白鸟。**  
*Tā kàn jiàn shuǐ lǐ yǒu yì zhī dà bái niǎo.*  
It saw a big white bird in the water.

---

### 美丽的发现 (The Beautiful Discovery)

**小鸭子仔细一看——那是它自己！**  
*Xiǎo yāzi zǐxì yí kàn——nà shì tā zìjǐ!*  
The duckling looked carefully—that was itself!

**它不再是丑小鸭了！**  
*Tā bú zài shì chǒu xiǎo yā le!*  
It was no longer an ugly duckling!

**它变成了一只美丽的白天鹅！**  
*Tā biàn chéng le yì zhī měilì de bái tiān'é!*  
It had become a beautiful white swan!

**这时，一群天鹅游过来。**  
*Zhè shí, yì qún tiān'é yóu guò lái.*  
Just then, a flock of swans swam over.

**"你好！你真美丽！跟我们一起玩吧！"**  
*"Nǐ hǎo! Nǐ zhēn měilì! Gēn wǒmen yìqǐ wán ba!"*  
"Hello! You're so beautiful! Come play with us!"

**天鹅非常高兴。**  
*Tiān'é fēicháng gāoxìng.*  
The swan was very happy.

**它终于找到了自己的家人！**  
*Tā zhōngyú zhǎo dào le zìjǐ de jiārén!*  
It finally found its own family!

---

### 故事的道理 (The Moral of the Story)

**不要因为外表就欺负别人。**  
*Búyào yīnwèi wàibiǎo jiù qīfu biérén.*  
Don't bully others because of their appearance.

**每个人都有自己的美丽。**  
*Měi gè rén dōu yǒu zìjǐ de měilì.*  
Everyone has their own beauty.

**要相信自己，耐心等待！**  
*Yào xiāngxìn zìjǐ, nàixīn děngdài!*  
Believe in yourself and wait patiently!

**有一天，你会发现自己的特别之处！**  
*Yǒu yì tiān, nǐ huì fāxiàn zìjǐ de tèbié zhī chù!*  
One day, you will discover what makes you special!
