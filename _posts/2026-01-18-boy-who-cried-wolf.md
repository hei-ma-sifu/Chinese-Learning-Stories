# 狼来了！
## The Boy Who Cried Wolf

---

### 放羊的小男孩 (The Shepherd Boy)

**从前，有一个小男孩。**  
*Cóngqián, yǒu yí gè xiǎo nánhái.*  
Once upon a time, there was a little boy.

**他每天在山上放羊。**  
*Tā měitiān zài shān shàng fàng yáng.*  
Every day he herded sheep on the mountain.

**放羊很无聊。他觉得一点儿也不好玩。**  
*Fàng yáng hěn wúliáo. Tā juéde yìdiǎnr yě bù hǎowán.*  
Herding sheep was very boring. He felt it wasn't fun at all.

**"我要做点儿有趣的事！"他想。**  
*"Wǒ yào zuò diǎnr yǒuqù de shì!" tā xiǎng.*  
"I want to do something interesting!" he thought.

---

### 第一次说谎 (The First Lie)

**突然，小男孩大喊："狼来了！狼来了！救命啊！"**  
*Tūrán, xiǎo nánhái dà hǎn: "Láng lái le! Láng lái le! Jiùmìng a!"*  
Suddenly, the little boy shouted: "Wolf! Wolf! Help!"

**村民们听到了，赶快跑上山。**  
*Cūnmín men tīng dào le, gǎnkuài pǎo shàng shān.*  
The villagers heard him and quickly ran up the mountain.

**"狼在哪里？狼在哪里？"**  
*"Láng zài nǎlǐ? Láng zài nǎlǐ?"*  
"Where's the wolf? Where's the wolf?"

**可是，山上没有狼！**  
*Kěshì, shān shàng méiyǒu láng!*  
But there was no wolf on the mountain!

**小男孩哈哈大笑："骗你们的！没有狼！哈哈哈！"**  
*Xiǎo nánhái hāhā dà xiào: "Piàn nǐmen de! Méiyǒu láng! Hāhāhā!"*  
The little boy laughed loudly: "I tricked you! There's no wolf! Hahaha!"

**村民们很生气，下山回家了。**  
*Cūnmín men hěn shēngqì, xià shān huí jiā le.*  
The villagers were very angry and went back down the mountain to go home.

---

### 第二次说谎 (The Second Lie)

**过了几天，小男孩又觉得无聊了。**  
*Guò le jǐ tiān, xiǎo nánhái yòu juéde wúliáo le.*  
A few days later, the little boy felt bored again.

**他又大喊："狼来了！狼来了！快来帮我！"**  
*Tā yòu dà hǎn: "Láng lái le! Láng lái le! Kuài lái bāng wǒ!"*  
He shouted again: "Wolf! Wolf! Come help me quickly!"

**村民们又跑上山来。**  
*Cūnmín men yòu pǎo shàng shān lái.*  
The villagers ran up the mountain again.

**"这次狼真的来了吗？"**  
*"Zhè cì láng zhēn de lái le ma?"*  
"Did the wolf really come this time?"

**还是没有狼！**  
*Háishì méiyǒu láng!*  
Still no wolf!

**小男孩又笑了："又骗到你们了！"**  
*Xiǎo nánhái yòu xiào le: "Yòu piàn dào nǐmen le!"*  
The little boy laughed again: "I tricked you again!"

---

### 狼真的来了！(The Wolf Really Came!)

**又过了几天。**  
*Yòu guò le jǐ tiān.*  
A few more days passed.

**这一天，一只大灰狼真的来了！**  
*Zhè yì tiān, yì zhī dà huī láng zhēn de lái le!*  
This day, a big gray wolf really came!

**小男孩很害怕！他大声喊："狼来了！狼来了！真的有狼！救命啊！"**  
*Xiǎo nánhái hěn hàipà! Tā dàshēng hǎn: "Láng lái le! Láng lái le! Zhēn de yǒu láng! Jiùmìng a!"*  
The little boy was very scared! He shouted loudly: "Wolf! Wolf! There really is a wolf! Help!"

**可是，这次没有人来帮他。**  
*Kěshì, zhè cì méiyǒu rén lái bāng tā.*  
But this time, no one came to help him.

**"又是那个小男孩在骗人吧。"村民们说。**  
*"Yòu shì nàge xiǎo nánhái zài piàn rén ba." Cūnmín men shuō.*  
"It's that little boy lying again," the villagers said.

**所以，没有人上山去帮他。**  
*Suǒyǐ, méiyǒu rén shàng shān qù bāng tā.*  
So, no one went up the mountain to help him.

---

### 故事的道理 (The Moral of the Story)

**说谎的人，没有人会相信。**  
*Shuōhuǎng de rén, méiyǒu rén huì xiāngxìn.*  
People who lie, no one will believe.

**诚实最重要！**  
*Chéngshí zuì zhòngyào!*  
Honesty is most important!

**要做一个诚实的好孩子！**  
*Yào zuò yí gè chéngshí de hǎo háizi!*  
Be an honest, good child!
